Correlations Discovered Between Smoking and Age

There is no significant correlation between the median age of a state and it's percentage of smokers. The percentage of smokers around the median age of 36-40 seems to be more clustered. West Virginia, Kentucky, Arkansas,Tennessee and Louisiana are the top five states with highest percentage of smokers, all above 23% that fall in this bracket.

The state of Utah seemed to be an outlier with the lowest percentage of smokers at just 9.7%.

This data alone does not indicate the reason why the median age of smokers is between 36-40, other factors would also need to be analysed.
